YBN Cordae

YBN Cordae is an American Rapper from Maryland. He is part of the YBN Collective alongside YBN Nahmir and YBN Almighty Jay.
Cordae is considered to be the lyrical forcefield of the YBN crew.
He is known for his songs "Old Niggas" and "My Name is".

The Lost Boy

The Lost Boy is YBN Cordae's official debut album.
Cordae's album was released in the Summer of 2019. Response from new and old fans of the artist were overwhelmingly positive. With features from the likes of Chance the Rapper and Meek Mill, the tracks were versitile and fresh.
